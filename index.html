About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Australian Migration

TrustLink Migration was founded by Sourav Kumar Jindal with a clear mission: to make Australian migration accessible, transparent, and stress-free for every client.

We specialise in admission guidance, student visa assistance, and visitor visa support. Our approach is personal — we treat every client's journey as unique, providing hands-on guidance from initial enquiry through to application and beyond.

Based in Adelaide, South Australia, we are committed to expanding our services to cover a wider range of migration pathways. Our MARA registration is currently in progress, reflecting our dedication to meeting the highest professional standards.
